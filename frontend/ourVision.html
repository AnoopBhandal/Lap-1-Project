Our Vision

This quiz aims to improve secondary school students engagement with non-STEM subjects. Focusing on history, geography and art, our quiz provides a fun and engaging way for students to review the infomation taught during lessons, allowing them to retain the knowledge they've learned.

Goal One

Due to focus on STEM subjects, it is often difficult to find time for non-STEM subjects. This quiz helps students retain knowledge taught in non-STEM lessons, allowing them to gain deeper knowledge of these subjects.

Goal Two

Lessons can often feel repetitive when they focus too much on textbooks. This quiz provides a fun alternative to help students who are struggling to remember all the information they are taught.

Goal Three

Parents find that their children are often overwhelmed by the amount of content they are given in lessons. This quiz helps students enjoy they content they are being taught.
